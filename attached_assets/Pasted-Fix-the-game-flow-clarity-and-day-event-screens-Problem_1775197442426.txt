Fix the game flow, clarity, and day event screens.

Problems to fix:
1. When all citizens are processed, the game leaves the desk empty and makes the player manually end the shift.
2. Day event screens (like the stock market shock) are too small, too wordy, and do not feel important.
3. Some warning labels are vague and confusing, such as "Suspicious line items".
4. The player does not clearly know what they are supposed to check or what each action means.
5. If the player goes into debt or cannot afford a choice, the game can get stuck and they cannot continue.

--------------------------------
1. AUTO-END THE SHIFT
--------------------------------

When all citizens are processed:
- automatically end the shift
- immediately transition to the end-of-day screen
- do NOT leave the desk empty
- do NOT require the player to manually click "End Shift" after the work is already done

--------------------------------
2. REVAMP DAY EVENT SCREENS
--------------------------------

Redesign all day-start event screens to be bold, simple, and readable.

Current issue:
- text is too small
- too many words
- not enough impact
- player has to squint

Use a much simpler format like:

DAY 3

MARKET CRASH

WAGES -30%
PENALTIES HIGHER

[ START SHIFT ]

Rules:
- maximum 3 short lines
- no paragraphs
- very large text
- strong contrast
- no tiny explanatory text

The player should understand the event in under 2 seconds.

--------------------------------
3. ADD A SHORT NEWSPAPER CUTSCENE
--------------------------------

Before major day events, add a very short world-event cutscene.

Use a stylized newspaper spinning onto the screen with a bold headline.

Example:
- newspaper spins in
- headline appears:
  "MARKET PANIC"
  or
  "MINISTRY WARNS OF CRASH"
- optional subheadline:
  "Wages reduced nationwide"

Then transition into the simple day screen.

Important:
- keep it short (2–4 seconds)
- make it readable
- do not use graphic or extreme disaster imagery
- use this to make the event feel important

--------------------------------
4. REPLACE VAGUE WARNINGS
--------------------------------

Remove unclear warnings like:
- "Suspicious line items"

Replace them with short, specific labels that tell the player what to look for.

Examples:
- INCOME TOO HIGH
- DEDUCTIONS UNUSUAL
- EMPLOYER UNKNOWN
- EXPENSES EXCESSIVE

The player should immediately understand what is suspicious.

--------------------------------
5. ADD PERMANENT PLAYER INSTRUCTIONS
--------------------------------

The player currently does not clearly know what to do.

Add a permanent minimal instruction panel on the gameplay screen that always shows:

CHECK
- NAME MATCH
- SSN MATCH
- INCOME MATCH

ACTIONS
- APPROVE = match
- REJECT = mismatch
- FREEZE = fraud

Rules:
- no paragraphs
- no long explanations
- keep it visible at all times
- make it easy to read at a glance

--------------------------------
6. FIX DEBT / SOFTLOCK
--------------------------------

The player must never get stuck.

If the player has no money or goes into debt:
- always allow them to continue
- allow skipping choices
- or allow negative balance
- but never trap the player on the screen

Possible warning:
NO FUNDS
CONTINUING WITH CONSEQUENCES

But the player must always be able to move on.

--------------------------------
7. READABILITY RULE
--------------------------------

All important text must be much larger.

Increase font size for:
- day event headlines
- warnings
- instruction labels
- key gameplay text

Nothing important should require squinting.

Goal:
Make the player always know:
- what happened in the world
- what to check
- what each action means
- what happens next

And make sure the game never stalls or traps the player.
